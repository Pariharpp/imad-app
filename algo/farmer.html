Soil Health Card 

	     What is a Soil Health Card?

        SHC is a printed report that a farmer will be handed over for each of his holdings. It will contain the status of his soil with respect
 to 12 parameters, namely N,P,K (Macro-nutrients) ; S (Secondary- nutrient) ; Zn, Fe, Cu, Mn, Bo (Micro - nutrients) ; and pH, EC
, OC (Physical parameters). Based on this, the SHC will also indicate fertilizer recommendations and soil amendment required for
 the farm.
